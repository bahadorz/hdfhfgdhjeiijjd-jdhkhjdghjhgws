Vowel reduction coach: О/А

Russian spelling can trick your eyes. Unstressed о and а are reduced. Right before the stressed syllable, at the beginning, and often at the end of a word they sound like a weak а. Farther from stress, they become an even quicker indistinct uh. The app now includes special drills for this: около, город, города, погода, хорошо, Новгород, Новослободская.

Rule of thumb: trust your ears more than the spelling. Do not pronounce every written О as a full “o”.
